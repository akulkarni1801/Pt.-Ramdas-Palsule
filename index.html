About

Pandit Ramdas Palsule is an internationally-acclaimed Tabla virtuoso from Pune, India.
Ramdas is a founder member and a Guru at Avartan Gurukul - a unique institution for studying Hindustani music in Pune.
He is also an A grade artist of All India Radio and Doordarshan.
